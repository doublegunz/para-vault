## About the Author {#about-the-author}

This book is written by **Gun Gun Priatna** and published under **qadrlabs**, a place for practical, build-something-real programming tutorials. The approach is simple: teach concepts at the moment they are needed, apply them immediately inside a real project, and always explain the reasoning behind the code, not just the syntax.

The same philosophy runs through everything published under qadrlabs. You do not learn a long list of disconnected features. You build a working application and understand why each piece is there.

You can find more tutorials, courses, and books at **qadrlabs.com**. A longer note about qadrlabs and the other products available to you is at the end of this book.
